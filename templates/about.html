This is only a personal project, and a very simple one at that. I'm often intrigued by seeing the tweets of my moots, who are often so passionate about certain topics. For the last year or so, I've seen so many of my moots talk mostly about AOT, and I thought, how often do they tweet about this topic. Maybe I should build a tool to try and find out.
Accounts I love and follow, such as Cr7RapRhymes, is a Ronaldo fan account, but using the tool you'll see how much rent free Messi lives in his head 😆

How the tool works
First, the tool gets the tweets from the user's public timeline (only tweets, I decided not to include rts, qrts and replies, for now) and breaks it down to individual words to search for nouns, proper nouns etc, and then investigates the frequency of that particular topic word in all the tweets.
Then it finds out the six most frequently appearing topic words (I thought it works okayishly till six), and uses Google charts to make a graph, cause why not.
